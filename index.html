Collection of ArtWork

Imagination Collection

This is a collection I made in my free time based around the visual representation of the cognitive sensation and phenomenon of imagination. I wanted to show the idea of how creative ideas are formed in three different styles. The right most image is about the crystallization of an idea and done in a realistic style in Corel Painter. The center image is about how the mind stretches to accommodate new ideas. It is done in a surrealistic style with PhotoShop. The left most image is about how the mind travels on a current when imagining and is directed by outside forces and I represented the mind as an abstract leaf on the wind or water. It's done with more traditional mediums of marker and pen. The left most is my favourite of the three because it looks like a lot of different things when rotated.
