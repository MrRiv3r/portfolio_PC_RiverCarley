Print Media

House Plant Handbook

This was a school assignment to create an informative book on a subject of our choosing. I had to research the subject, illustrate the images, design the layout of the booklet, and print and assemble the physical copy. The booklet in its entirety can be seen here.

No Poverty Campaign and Magazine Spread

This was a school project about the UN’s Global Action Goals. The goal I chose to focus on was poverty. Everyone in the class created a magazine spread with infographics, and they were all compiled into a single magazine. For the second half of the project, we had to create an infographic ad campaign. Both the magazine and the ad campaign can be seen here.

DomoDeco Custom Font Poster

This was a school project where we created a custom font of our choosing and had to produce a number of printed accompaniments. I'm particularly fond of the final part of the project, which involved creating a poster. The poster is double-sided and can be displayed horizontally or vertically depending on which side is up. I used FontLab 8 to create the font, InDesign for the layout, and ZBrush to model the silver mask. The poster can be seen here.

Book Suggester Website

Lecture Series WebPoster

A project I completed in the second half of second year at OCADU. It's an interactive poster for a lecture series in the class Research Methods for Graphic Design. One of the main requirements was ensuring it adapted fluidly to all screen sizes and orientations. It can be seen here.

Logos

These are all freelance projects I completed while in school.

StoryMode — The left-most logo was designed for a video-game-inspired clothing brand. This project went through many iterations—this is one of about twenty final versions. The challenge was creating a design that read as “video game” without infringing on existing intellectual property.

Bright Black — A furniture refinishing company that paints pieces in bright colours and applies a black wash so that just a hint of the colour shows through. The challenge was communicating the craftsperson's technique visually within the logo.

Aureole — A video editing service. This project was challenging because the client had a very clear idea, along with a detailed 3D AI-generated image they wanted interpreted as a logo. I feel this logo could still be improved, as it has functional issues in terms of detail and contrast.
